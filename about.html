The Secret Sauce

Welcome to Passta. We believe that security shouldn't be bland. In a world of random character strings that look like spilled soup, we serve up passwords that are easy to say, but hard to guess.

The Recipe

Every Passta password is cooked to order based on your selected Portion Size (10 to 18 characters):

  • Fresh Ingredients: An Adjective and Noun pair, perfectly scaled from 4 to 8 letters each.
  • The Vintage: Two unique numbers added to the end (e.g. 92).
  • The Spice: One hidden symbol substitution to satisfy security requirements.
  • CamelCase: The second word is capitalized for readability.

We ensure every password contains exactly one special character for maximum security.

Our Promise

We cook tableside. Passta runs entirely in your browser. We do not store, save, or see the passwords you generate. Once you leave this page, the pasta is gone forever.
